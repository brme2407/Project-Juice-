Tequila Sunrise

1.5 Fluid Ounces Jigger Tequila
3/4 Cup Freshly Squeezed Orange Juice
1 Cup Ice
1.5 Fluid Ounces Jigger Grenadine Syrup
1 Slice Orange
1 Maraschino Cherry

Directions

1. Pour Tequila and Orange Juice into shaker
2. Stir/Shake well
3. Pour Ice and Tequila/Orange Juice into 12 oz. glass
4. Slowly pour in Grenadine Syrup and let it sink to the bottom
5. Add Orange Slice and Maraschino Cherry for Garnish
6. Serve
