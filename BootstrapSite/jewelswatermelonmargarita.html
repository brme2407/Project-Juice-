Jewels Watermelon Margarita

1.5 Cup White Sugar
1.5 Cup Water
2 Cups Ice cubed or crushed
3 Strips Orange Zest
2 Cups Watermelon cubed
3/4 Cup White Tequila
1/4 Cup Lime Juice
1 Lime cut into wedges
Salt or Sugar for rimming glass

Directions

1. Combine Sugar, Water, and Orange Zest in a small saucepan
2. Bring to a boil, continuously stirring
3. Let simmer until all sugar is dissolved
4. Remove from heat and cool
5. Blend Watermelon cubes until pureed
6. Stir puree, Tequila, Lime Juice, and Syrup from Step 1 into large pitcher
7. Fill small saucer with salt/sugar
8. Rub rim of margarita glass with lime and gently dip into saucer
9. Fill rimmed glass with Ice and Margarita mixture
10. Garnish with Lime and Serve
